# Before Quiz
- Arrive early and log into a desktop. No need to open any app or browser.
- Put all your items inside your bag and send the bag at the front of the lab
    room. You may keep pen or pencil with you, but not any paper or resource.

# Beginning Quiz
- Unlock the desktop and enter your ID and programming language information
    into the console window.
- Wait a few moments, and finally ensure that the newly appearing checkers
    windows in the terminal show some score (“score = 0/1” for example).
- Find the tasks inside folders named like "_TID" (the task ID, an underscore
    followed by 3 alphanumeric characters). The whole system in such a folder
    is called a checker. Find checker related instructions inside the file
    "_TID_Instruction.md".
- Turn on Auto save (VS Code: File --> Auto save).

# During Quiz
- Do not keep any mobile phones nearby.
- Do not open/view/copy any pre-written code.
- Do not use any internet service.
- Do not keep/open any web browser.
- Do not open/use any AI/GPT/AutoCoding/Chatting service.

# Ending Quiz
- Ensure to save your codes before the end of the quiz.

# After Quiz
- Do not leave the lab before ensuring your submission and attendance.
